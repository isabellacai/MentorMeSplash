MentorMe connects
LGBTQ and minority mentees with mentors

It is a safe place to anonymously discuss issues with a trusted peer or adult.

Fast

MentorMe matches the mentor and mentee based primarily on how long they've been waiting since clicking the "search" button.

Anyone, Anywhere

Need someone to talk to? Want to share your experiences and advice with a struggling human being? You've come to the right place! MentorMe is for anyone, anywhere.

Privacy Guaranteed

We do not store any personal information whatsover. No one, including us, knows the identity of the mentee (besides the mentees themselves, of course).

100% Free

MentorMe was created by Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute students Isabella Cai and Logan Shire at HackRPI 2014. It has won the prize for Best Humanitarian Hack and is being distributed completely free of charge.
